A serious job search is a full-time operational problem.

Ten active leads at different stages. A tailored resume per role. Personalised outreach to hiring managers and recruiters. Interview prep across multiple companies. Behavioral story libraries. Follow-up queues. All happening in parallel — with a spreadsheet and hope.

There is no good tool for this. JobHunt OS is that tool.

What it does

Three engines. One pipeline.

📄
Resume Engine
AI tailors your base resume to any JD, evaluates it (scored /10), and runs a 13-point preflight check before PDF generation. Always one page.
✉️
Outreach Engine
Drafts personalised HM + TA messages, builds a daily send queue, tracks full lifecycle (connect → accepted → messaged → replied), and schedules follow-up nudges.
🧠
Interview Prep
Per-company prep workspace: JD gap analysis, round-by-round guide, behavioral story library (STAR+C), and a daily AI brief synthesising today's focus.
📊
Pipeline Tracker
Master applications table with status tracking (Outreach → Applied → Interviewing → Offer). Syncs to Google Sheets. Response rate analytics coming.

How it works

Agentic from the start.

1
Upload your base resume + paste a JD
An Opus agent tailors your resume to the role. A second independent agent scores it out of 10 and gives the top 3 fixes. A preflight agent validates formatting before PDF generation.
2
Find the hiring manager. Draft outreach.
AI drafts personalised HM + TA messages. Every morning, a review surface aggregates all "ready to send" messages across every active lead — grouped by tier.
3
Send. Track. Follow up.
LinkedIn integration sends connect requests and DMs (with your approval). Follow-up queues nudge you on acceptance checks and reply chasers — no lead goes cold.
4
Prep for the interview.
Per-company prep workspace, AI-generated rubrics, and a daily brief that tells you exactly where to focus today across all active interview loops.
